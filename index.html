Roll dice in Hangouts

brought to you +Richard Wooding

I am proud to introduce "Roll The Dice" a Google+ Hangout Extension that allows you roll dice in Hangouts.

It's open-ended what you do with results of the roll, you could use it for:

  • Conflict resolution
  • Choosing teams
  • Dice games

To install use the button below (which starts a hangout with extension), or find it listed under "Apps" in an hangout where it is in use.

Thanks to +George Sepich and +Larry Fournillier for ideas.
